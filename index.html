Gears

A gear or cogwheel is a rotating machine part having cut teeth or, in the case of a cogwheel, inserted teeth (called cogs), which mesh with another toothed part to transmit torque. Geared devices can change the speed, torque, and direction of a power source.

Hydraulics

Hydraulics is a technology and applied science using engineering, chemistry, and other sciences involving the mechanical properties and use of liquids. At a very basic level, hydraulics is the liquid counterpart of pneumatics, which concerns gases.

Spares

A spare part, spare, service part, repair part, or replacement part, is an interchangeable part that is kept in an inventory and used for the repair or replacement of failed units.

About

Adhie Gears and Hydraulics.

  • 1995

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The company was started in the year 1995 in the name of MICRO ENGINEERING ENTERPRISES. The founder of the company is Mr.S.Dorai Raju. The company was initiated with job works for many industries and for IISC (INDIAN INSTITUTE OF SCIENCE).

  • 1996

    An Agency is Born

    In the year 1996 our company was involved into the earth -moving field by a Tamil Nadu earth-moving dealer, who came up with to our company for manufacture control valve spool for L&T CK90 machine which was a challenging job, as that particular spool is not available in the market. Whereas the own Machine Manufacturing Company required a minimum of 6 months time to make out the product. As we manufactured the spool within 7 days and after fitting it to the control vale it started working very very good and we had a warm welcome to the Earth-moving filed, and customers were very satisfied and started to encourage in many other earth-moving products. Than we started manufacturing hydraulic pump parts for uchitha pump, Kawasaki pump and all parts for track motor and swing motor. In this way we entered the manufacturing earth moving parts.

  • 2005

    Transition to Full Service

    Soon after we started to analyse the market we found that travel device of ex 70 , PC71 and PC130 had problem in the RV-Gear system, than we our self designed a travel device which entirely rocked the earth-moving market and it is running successfully, for this product production we stated one more unit in the name of of Sre Athe Enterprises in the year 2005 & started to supply our products throughout India.

  • We developed our factory into this manufacturing industry and started to capture Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Andra Pradesh, Kerala, Mumbai, Kolkata, Delhi, Gujarat and Andaman Nicobar market and having a good hold on Indian market due to the best quality with guarantee supply assurance of the manufactured products with a highly customer satisfaction. We are also in exports to other countries and having a showroom in Australia Sydney
